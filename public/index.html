Syllabus Overview

Questions will be based on the syllabus of the junior class in each category (up to the first half of the academic year), ensuring fairness for all participants.

For example, in Category D (Classes 9 & 10), questions will be based on the Class 9 syllabus only.

In addition to academic topics, the paper will include:

  • Logical Reasoning
  • Mathematical Thinking
  • Scientific Aptitude
  • Problem-Solving & Pattern Recognition
